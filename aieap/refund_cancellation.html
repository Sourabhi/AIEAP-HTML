Refund and Cancellation Policies
  1. If immigration authorities at a Canadian Embassy or High Commission Office issue a letter denying the request for a study permit, and the AIEAP Office receives a copy of the letter of denial accompanied by formal notification of withdrawal or request for deferral within 10 business days of the beginning of a school year, then the total fees less $200.00 will be refunded or deferred. Fees paid in advance for future school year(s) will be refunded or deferred in full.

  2. If notice of cancellation is received less than 28 days before the commencement date of your course, 60 % tuition will be refunded.

  3. No refund is payable after you have commenced studying at AIEAP.

  4. Course fees are not transferable to another person or institution.

  5. In the unlikely circumstance that AIEAP must cancel a course, all tuition fees will be refunded in full.
