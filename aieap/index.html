The American Institute of English for Academic Preparation --the Pathway to Higher Education in Canada and the USA.

Now with your Low TOEFL or IELTS score, you still have the unforeseeable opportunity to materialize your dream!! Your dream of higher studies in the English Speaking Countries. AIEAP will prepare students with their Academic English Competency in order to be accepted, and be successful in their college and university programs in Canada and the USA.

Location: AIEAP is situated in the heart of the GTA Ontario, Canada. The City of Toronto, the economic hub, the provincial capital is known for its vibrant ethnic diversity, economic opportunities, tourist landmarks, and recreational activities. The City provides a safe and stimulating environment in which overseas students have boundless opportunities to pursue academic, professional and cultural accomplishments.
