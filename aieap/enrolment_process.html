Enrolment Process
  1. Read carefully our Condition of Acceptance and our Refund and Cancellation Policies.

  2. Complete the Online Enrolment Form - we will reply to check your request and availability.

  3. Forward payment to our bank account. Please see Payment Methods.

  4. Pay $ 75 CD. Non-refundable application fee.

  5. Bank statement showing sufficient funds (Tuition and Fees).

  6. Official high school transcript and or college transcript.

  7. A copy of valid passport.

  8. IELTS or TOEFL score (For visa this is advisable).

  9. Students studying on student visas must also purchase medical insurance for the duration of their course â€“see Insurance below.

  10. When we process your enrolment, we will return a Schedule of Fees and a Confirmation of Enrolment (COE). Email us for free visa assistance.
